I have always had a love of visual communication, design aesthetics, and graphics technology, though my formal education was in the natural and social sciences. Rediscovering my passion for design led me to take the leap from underemployed academic to creative entrepreneur.

My e-commerce site is in the works and will be launching very soon, so stay tuned. Starting this summer, I will be offering custom design services and selling creative products locally in Pittsburgh, PA.

In addition to my design work, I write about politics of the digital economy and publish a weekly newsletter featuring thoughtfully curated articles and commentary on this topic.
